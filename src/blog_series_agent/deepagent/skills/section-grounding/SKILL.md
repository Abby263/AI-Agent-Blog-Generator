# Section Grounding Skill

## Required Evidence

Each substantial section needs:

- At least one exact clickable source link when grounded research is enabled.
- Source notes that explain how the source supports the section.
- No invented article titles, company references, years, or benchmark numbers.
- No prose that says research failed inside the final article.

## Fallback Behavior

If section-level search is thin, reuse validated chapter-level source links and
write from the section plan. Do not expose internal retrieval failures to the
reader.
