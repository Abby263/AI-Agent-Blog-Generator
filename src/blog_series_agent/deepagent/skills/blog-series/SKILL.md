# Blog Series Skill

## Workflow

1. Start with the learning journey, not isolated post ideas.
2. Make every chapter depend on earlier context and prepare the reader for the
   next chapter.
3. Keep overlap low by assigning each chapter a distinct production question.
4. Preserve a book-like arc: requirements, data, features, training, evaluation,
   serving, monitoring, feedback, scale, tradeoffs, case study, readiness.

## Quality Bar

- Each chapter must have a concrete operational problem.
- Every dense list needs a synthesis paragraph.
- The final article should read as one chapter, not a bundle of notes.
- The conclusion should name what the reader can now design or evaluate.
